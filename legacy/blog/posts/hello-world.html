Hello, world!

Posted on 8th June, 2020 by Harshit Doshi

Convention? Welcome?

"Hello, world!" is a programming convention. It is the first program you'd write when you start coding for the first time or you are getting started with a new language or technology. And, to abide by the convention, I am starting this blog with a "Hello, world!" post.

If you know me as a computer science student, you know that I have always wanted to write a blog. But, writing has never been my strong suit. After years of delay, here it is. What spurred this sudden death of procrastination, you might ask?

Well, today, 8th of June, 2020, I start my first job. I am starting as a Systems Engineer at Cerner Corporation. I finish a half-year long internship and transition to a full-time role. This made me look back upon the exciting journey it took for me to reach here. I have never been so motivated.

So, here it is. Hello, world! Welcome to my tiny spot on the Internet.
