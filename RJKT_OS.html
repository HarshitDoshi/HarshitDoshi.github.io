What is RJKT_OS?

RJKT_OS is an initiative to promote the development of open source computing and to promote contributions towards the development of open source solutions. RJKT_OS is a platform for people to learn, teach and share the awesome development they do for the open source community. Here, at RJKT_OS, we have meet-ups, coding sessions, talks, discussions, food, and fun! Welcome to a world where computing is open!.
